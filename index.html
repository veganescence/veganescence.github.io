About us

A member of The Vegan Society, we are a new company that believes that a vegan life can be fragrant and stylish as well as cruelty-free.

Our candles offer a cleaner, purer burn for a healthier environment, and have been developed using soy wax from sustainable sources and vegan-friendly fragrances.

We use a combination of safe, synthetic ingredients for a stronger scent and essential oils to calm, relax and energise.

We use traditional cotton wicks in our smaller candles and also wooden wicks in larger candles that give a pleasant 'crackle' sound when lit.

Upcoming events

These are the events we'll be attending - please take a look and see if we'll be anywhere near you, and come along to say hello.

  1. Peterborough - Saturday 4th May 2019
    Cathedral Square, PE1 1XH     

    The Peterborough Vegan Market will be held on Cathedral Square located right in the centre of Peterborough, perfectly central so you can just hop off the high street straight into the market.
  3. Cathedral Square is a quick bus/taxi ride or short walk from the train station. 

    As well as VEGANESCENCE and our vegan candles, the organiser www.veganmarkets.co.uk says:

    "Whether you're vegan, vegetarian or just interested we're sure you'll enjoy the market and find lots of things you love! The Peterborough Vegan Market will be packed with stalls selling a huge a variety of vegan products. We'll have everything from amazing vegan food from around the world to luxury vegan cosmetics and from ethical clothing to interesting charities."

  1. Cambridge - Sunday 5th May 2019
    The Guildhall, Market Square, Cambridge CB2 3QJ     

    The Cambridge Vegan Market will be held in the wonderful Guildhall, located right in the centre of Cambridge, next to the market place.
  2. The venue has disabled lift access and lots of parking nearby.
  3. Unfortunately dogs are not permitted within the venue.
  5. Tickets will be available on the door and will be £2 for adults and free for children. You will be able to pay by card on the door. Doors will open at 10:30am and close at 4:00pm.
