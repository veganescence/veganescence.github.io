ABOUT US

We are a new company that believe life can be fragrant and stylish as well as cruelty-free.

Our candles have been developed using soy wax from sustainable sources and vegan-friendly fragrances.

We use a combination of safe, synthetic ingredients for a stronger scent and essential oils that can calm, relax and energise.

We use traditional cotton wicks in our smaller candles and also wooden wicks in larger candles that give a pleasant 'crackle' sound when lit.

Our current range includes:

  1. FIG AND FRANKINCENSE - a festive combination of frankincence base notes, snowberry and Christmas fig middle notes and a hint of lime to sharpen the senses.
  2. OAK AND CASSIS - a robust combination including tobacco and fig with a few drops of mandarin to remind us that summer will be here again.
  3. ORANGEFLOWER AND MALLORCAN LEMON - we source ingredients on our travels, and this heady combination is reminiscent of faraway places. A sweet and sharp mix.
  4. CEDARWOOD AND SEA-SALT - a robust, wild mix with heavy cedarwood base notes and uplifting seasalt.
