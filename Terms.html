Terms of Service, and Privacy Policy

Last updated: 14 November 2018
VEGANESCENCE is an independent vegan candlemaker, committed to selling great quality, great smelling candles at great prices, whilst adhering to vegan principles. Welcome to our website.
Respecting your privacy and safeguarding your data
VEGANESCENCE is committed to upholding your privacy and taking care of your personal data. This policy outlines what, why and how VEGANESCENCE collects, processes and stores your personal data securely in compliance with the General Data Protection Regulation (25 May 2018).
What personal data does VEGANESCENCE collect from you?
When signing up to VEGANESCENCE mailing list you will be asked for your name and your email address.
What other data does VEGANESCENCE collect?
Cookies
When browsing our website www.veganescence.com anonymous data is collected by cookies. A cookie is a small piece of data that is sent from VEGANESCENCE’s web server to your browser. It is stored on your hard drive. A cookie cannot read data off your hard disk other than the cookie itself, files created by other websites, and will not damage your system. You can reset your browser to refuse cookies or alert you when a cookie is being sent. VEGANESCENCE does not use cookies to identify you.
How does VEGANESCENCE use your data?
VEGANESCENCE collects your data to allow you to receive marketing e-mails from VEGANESCENCE about upcoming products or events that we feel will be of interest to you.
How does VEGANESCENCE collect your data?
You choose to sign up to the VEGANESCENCE mailing list either via VEGANESCENCE’s online sign-up form at www.veganescence.com or via the hard copy sign up forms displayed at the Point of Sale of VEGANESCENCE products.
How does VEGANESCENCE store your data?
Your data is held securely in a controlled access mail database, in compliance with GDPR rules or a password protected spreadsheet, held on a secure VEGANESCENCE non-networked computer.
How do you update the data VEGANESCENCE holds on you?
VEGANESCENCE endeavours to keep your personal data accurate and up to date. If you become aware of errors or inaccuracies, please e-mail info@veganescence.com with “UPDATE MY RECORDS” in the e-mail subject line.
What are your rights?
- Your rights: What does it mean
- Right of Access: To know if VEGANESCENCE have your data and to see it.
- Right to Rectify: To correct your data if you believe it is inaccurate.
- Right of Portability: The right to request a copy of your data in a common machine readable format that can be transferred to another organisation or database.
- Right of Erasure: To have some or all of your data delete (where legally possible) - Right not to be Profiled: The right not be subject to a decision based on automated processing.
- Right to Restrict Processing: To opt out of processing your data in a particular way without necessarily deleting it.
- Right to withdraw consent: If you have provided consent, you may withdraw this at any time.
- Right to Object: You have the right to register and objection if an organisation continues to process your data or send you direct marketing on the grounds of scientific/historical research or legitimate interests or public interest.
You can exercise your rights at any time by contacting VEGANESCENCE’s Data Controller at info@veganescence.com
Who does VEGANESCENCE share your data with?
VEGANESCENCE does not share your data with anyone.
How do I unsubscribe from e-mail from VEGANESCENCE?
If you no longer wish to receive e-mail communications from VEGANESCENCE, send an e-mail to info@veganescence.com with “PERMANENTLY DELETE” in the e-mail subject line.
Retention Period
The data VEGANESCENCE has stored is reviewed every 12 months and either deleted, archived or consent for it to be kept for another 12 months is sought. Personal data processed for any purpose(s) shall not be kept for longer than is deemed necessary.
Further Information
Should you have any questions on anything in this policy please e-mail VEGANESCENCE’s data controller on info@veganescence.com
